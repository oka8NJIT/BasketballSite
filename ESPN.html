What Is Basketball?

ESPN:


ESPN is an American multinational basic cable sports channel owned by ESPN Inc., owned jointly by The Walt Disney Company and Hearst Communication. ESPN shows scoreboards for current and previous games, airs the games on television, and posts articles from analysts on all sports.

Basketball on ESPN:


ESPN shows current and previous scoreboards all throughout the history of the NBA. It shows team statistics, standings, and advanced stats on all players.
