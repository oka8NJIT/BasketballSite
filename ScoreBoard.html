What Is Basketball?

How to Score:


The basic way to score in basketball is to shoot the basketball through the hoop. Depending where a player is on the court, baskets can count for 1, 2, or 3 points.

What is a Scoreboard?:


A scoredboard is a large board reflecting the score of a match in multiple different sports. In Basketball, the scoreboard also shows timeouts, fouls, and whether a team is in the bonus or not.
